About the Hackathon

The ESTIAM Hackathon is an exciting event where students come together to solve real-world problems through technology. Participants will have the opportunity to collaborate in teams, develop innovative solutions, and present their projects to a panel of judges.

Event Details:

  • Date: July 1st, 2024
  • Location: ESTIAM Campus, Paris
  • Duration: 48 hours
  • Teams: 4-6 members

Challenges:

  • Cybersecurity solutions for modern threats
  • Developing applications using NFC technology
  • Innovative uses of AI in everyday applications
